About Me

Hello!

Greetings! I'm Zenki Whiteley, an aspiring junior full-stack developer with a unique journey that spans from the meticulous world of civil design to the hands-on artistry of car detailing.

Hobbies

I have a passion for electronics, tinkering around and designing my own devices, one of which being my very own home made SLAM Lidar scanner. This device fires of near infra-red light to measure distances, inorder to create point clouds of its local environment. This extends into my other interest of digital twins, as I can use these points clouds to create near identical copies of places that I have been. Amongst all the designs and electronics, I also enjoy a plethora of different physical activities, being somewhat of an avid tennis player, playing weekly if I ever get the chance.
